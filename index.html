Our Story

At InvitoAI, we believe that every special moment deserves a unique and memorable invitation. Our journey began with a simple idea: to revolutionize the way people invite their loved ones to celebrate life's milestones. Frustrated by the limitations of traditional paper invitations and generic digital options, our founders envisioned a platform that combines creativity, technology, and personalization.

Launched in 2023, InvitoAI emerged as a solution for individuals seeking to create stunning, animated video invitations effortlessly. Our mission is to empower users to craft invitations that not only inform but also captivate and delight recipients.
